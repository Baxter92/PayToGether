
About DealTogether
DealTogether is a group buying platform designed to make everyday products more accessible through the power of collective action.
We start from a simple observation: prices are often high not because of the actual cost of the products, but because of intermediaries, insufficient volumes, and a lack of coordination between buyers. DealTogether changes this dynamic.
Our platform allows individuals to group together to buy as a group, and thus:
benefit from lower negotiated prices
access quality products at a better price
create a community of supportive buyers

🔗 How it works
DealTogether connects three players:
Buyers : join or initiate group purchases
The sellers offer volume discounts.
The platform : facilitates, secures and structures transactions
When a minimum number of buyers is reached, the prize is unlocked for everyone.

🌍 Our vision
We believe in a smarter and fairer model of consumption:
less waste
more purchasing power
a direct relationship with suppliers
DealTogether is not just about selling: we are building a collaborative ecosystem where everyone wins.

🤝 For whom?
New arrivals with no purchase history
Families looking to reduce their expenses
Organized communities (associations, local groups)
Responsible consumers

DealTogether is more than a platform: it's a new way to buy, together.
