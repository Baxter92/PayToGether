
Conditions for sellers
Last updated: April 1, 2026
1. Registration
Sellers must provide:
Legal information
Compliant products
Logistics capacity

2. Obligations
The seller agrees to:
Fulfill orders
Meet deadlines
Maintaining quality

3. Commission
DealTogether applies a commission on each sale negotiated directly with the seller.

4. Payment
Payments are made after:
Full confirmation of the deal

5. Disputes
The seller is responsible for:
manage returns
resolve complaints

6. Termination
DealTogether may suspend a seller in case of non-compliance.

7. Quality
A quality charter must be respected at all times.
