
Supplier Charter
At DealTogether , quality and trust are essential. As a supplier, you commit to respecting the following standards:

🏆 Product Quality
Products as described
Safety standards respected
No counterfeit or dangerous products

⏱️ Deadlines
Adherence to announced deadlines
Proactive communication in case of delay

📦 Delivery
Secure packaging
Reliable logistics
Order tracking

🤝 Customer service
Responsiveness to requests
Returns and complaints management
Professional attitude

🔍 Transparency
Clear product information
Prices justified
No deceptive practices

⚠️ Disrespect
Failure to comply with this charter may result in:
suspension
delisting
termination of the partnership

Our goal is to guarantee a flawless experience for everyone.
