
Refund Policy
Last updated: April 1, 2026
1. Group cancellation
If the purchase threshold is not reached:
The order is cancelled.
No payment is debited or a refund is issued.

2. Non-conforming products
You can request a refund if:
the product is defective
The product does not match the description

3. Deadlines
Requests must be made within 7 days of receipt .

4. Process
Contact support
Provide evidence (photos, description)
Analysis within 5 business days

5. Refund method
Refund via the original payment method

6. Exceptions
Some products may be non-refundable (indicated on the product page).

7. Contact
For any questions: contact@hanacalgary.ca
