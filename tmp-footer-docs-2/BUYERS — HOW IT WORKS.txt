
How it works
DealTogether lets you buy better and cheaper through group buying. Here's how to take full advantage of the platform:

1. 🔍 Discover the offers
Browse the offers available on the platform:
everyday products
goods in bulk
exclusive negotiated offers
Each offer indicates:
the current price at dealTogether
the price displayed on the current market
the number of buyers required

2. 👥 Join a group purchase
Select an offer and join an existing group or start your own:
The more buyers there are
the lower the price

3. 💳 Book & pay
You validate a deal and confirm the payment.
Your payment will be processed subject to the deal being completed.
Payment is validated when the deal is complete with the number of buyers required by the seller.

4. 🚀 Offer activation
When the required number of buyers is reached:
The final prize has been awarded.
The order is confirmed.

5. 📦 Delivery or pickup
You will receive your product:
at home
or at a collection point
The delivery times are specified by the seller.

🔒 Security and guarantee
secure payments
verified sellers
Customer support available

💡 Tip
Invite your friends and family to join your group to unlock the best prices faster.
